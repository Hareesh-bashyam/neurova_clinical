# Clinical Validation Summary

## Overview
This document summarizes the clinical validity of the standardized
psychometric instruments used within Neurova Clinical Engine.

## Instruments Used
- PHQ-9
- GAD-7
- PSS-10
- MDQ
- AUDIT
- STOP-BANG
- ASRS (if applicable)

## Validation Basis
Each instrument included is:
- Publicly available
- Widely validated in peer-reviewed literature
- Used globally in clinical screening contexts

## Scoring
Scoring rules are deterministic, versioned, and based on original
instrument publications.

## Note
Neurova does not modify question wording or scoring thresholds.
