# Neurova Clinical Engine — Intended Use

## Device Type
Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) — Class B (India)

## Intended Use
Neurova Clinical Engine is intended to be used by trained healthcare staff
within licensed healthcare facilities to administer standardized,
validated mental health screening instruments.

The software:
- Collects patient self-reported responses
- Computes standardized scores using validated scoring rules
- Generates structured screening summaries for clinician review

## NOT Intended For
- Diagnosis
- Treatment
- Medication recommendation
- Crisis intervention
- Standalone clinical decision-making

## Intended Users
- Psychiatrists
- Clinical Psychologists
- Trained healthcare staff under clinician supervision

## Intended Setting
- Hospitals, clinics, and healthcare centers with access to mental health professionals
Final clinical interpretation and treatment decisions


## Clinical Responsibility
Final clinical interpretation and treatment decisions
remain solely the responsibility of the licensed clinician.
